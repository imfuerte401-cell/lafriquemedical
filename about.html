About Us

Bringing Doctors Closer to Every Ghanaian

We believe that quality healthcare is a right, not a privilege. L'Afrique Medical Alert was created to bridge the gap between patients and medical professionals using technology.

Our Story

From a Vision to a Mission

L'Afrique Medical Alert was born out of the daily realities many Ghanaians face: long travel distances to clinics, overcrowded waiting rooms, high transport costs, and delayed care that can turn minor conditions into serious health emergencies.

As healthcare professionals, we saw an opportunity to use technology to connect patients directly with licensed doctors via phone or video, reducing barriers and bringing quality medical care closer to every community.

Today, we combine teleconsultations, wearable medical alert devices, and community health screenings to create a complete healthcare ecosystem that works for patients whether they are in Accra or in rural regions.

Our Values

What Guides Us Every Day
