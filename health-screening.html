Preventive Care

Community Health Screenings

We bring the clinic to your neighbourhood. Proactive testing is the best way to manage your health and catch issues before they become emergencies.

Why Early Screening Matters

In Ghana, many chronic conditions like hypertension and diabetes go undiagnosed until they reach a critical stage. Our mobile screening teams partner with local pharmacies and community centres to provide accessible testing for everyone.

Our screenings are designed to be fast, non-invasive, and informative, providing you with immediate results and professional medical advice on the spot.

Testing Services

What We Screen For

Vital Signs Check

Comprehensive assessment of your body's most basic functions.

  • Blood Pressure Monitoring
  • Heart Rate Variability
  • Oxygen Saturation
  • Body Temperature Check

Metabolic Screening

Early detection for the most common chronic conditions in West Africa.

  • Fasting Blood Glucose
  • BMI and Fat Percentage
  • Waist-to-Hip Ratio
  • Nutritional Consultation

Specialized Tests

Additional screenings based on community needs and risk factors.

  • Vision & Hearing Basic Tests
  • Respiratory Function (Peak Flow)
  • Malaria RDT (when applicable)
  • Hepatitis B & C Screening
