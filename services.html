Our Services

Comprehensive Medical Alert Solutions

From virtual consultations to emergency alert devices, we provide end-to-end healthcare services designed for Ghanaian communities.

Virtual Care

Teleconsultations

Connect with licensed physicians via high-quality video or voice call. Our platform matches you with the right specialist for your health concern, ensuring timely and accurate medical advice.

  • Video and voice consultations
  • E-prescriptions and referrals
  • Follow-up care coordination
  • Secure, private consultations
Emergency Response

Medical Alert System

Our wearable medical alert device gives you peace of mind with one-touch emergency response. When you press the SOS button, our team is notified instantly along with your GPS location and vital signs.

  • One-touch SOS emergency button
  • Real-time GPS location sharing
  • Heart rate and vitals monitoring
  • 24/7 monitoring centre
Preventive Care

Health Screening Programs

We organise regular community health screenings partnering with local clinics and pharmacies. Early detection of conditions like hypertension, diabetes, and other chronic diseases saves lives.

  • Blood pressure and glucose testing
  • BMI and general health assessments
  • Free community outreach events
  • Digital health records and follow-up
Ongoing Care

Remote Patient Monitoring

For patients managing chronic conditions, our remote monitoring service connects your health devices to our platform so your care team can track your vitals and respond to changes in real time.

  • Bluetooth-connected health devices
  • Automated health alerts for care teams
  • Personal health dashboard
  • Chronic disease management plans
